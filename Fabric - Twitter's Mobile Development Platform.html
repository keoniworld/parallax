The Pulse of All Your Apps

Developers pour their heart & soul into building their amazing apps and want to keep a constant pulse on their apps once out in the wild. While there are many tools today that help you track crashes or user behavior within your apps, no one solution exists that keeps all these critical data in a single, intuitive dashboard -- until now.

With Fabric, you’ll have instant access to the same features you love within Crashlytics and more. Get a snapshot of your app’s health in real-time, understand what’s truly important and fix the most prevalent crashes -- all within the same web dashboard. No more jumping from dashboards to dashboards across different services.

When you’re away from your desk or on the beach, you can rest easy knowing that we are always looking out for your app. The Fabric mobile app can send you push notifications whenever something is critically affecting your app.

Proactive Alerting. Now.

Intelligent Alerts in Real-time.

Nothing is more important than getting timely and actionable data. Same as Crashlytics, we've built a layer of intelligent post-processing to alert you about key events within your app as they happen.

No need to keep revisiting your dashboard to stay on top of your apps. Our real-time notification system will proactively alert you when key events happen in your app. You’ll get actionable crash reports, performance metrics and our intelligent analyses -- all delivered right to your inbox.

Our servers and infrastructure now power thousands of the world's top apps and see nearly 5 billion app sessions everyday. No matter the size of your data flow, our infrastructure can handle the load.

Powerful Developer Account.

Many mobile SDKs have emerged over the last several years – each with the goal of solving a specific problem. As a result, many developers today are facing a huge time sink when building their apps: integrating numerous service-backed SDKs and managing their dependencies -- all while dealing with complicated accounts setup from multiple services.

With Fabric, you’ll have a single, dedicated developer account with complete access to best-in-class mobile SDKs. Simply add a few lines of code and start coding your app right away. We'll even provision your keys for you.
